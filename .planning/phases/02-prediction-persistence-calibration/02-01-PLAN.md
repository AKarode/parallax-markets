<objective>
Persist every prediction the system makes in a new `prediction_log` DuckDB table with full context (probability, reasoning, news, cascade state), and add run_id correlation to signal_ledger entries.

Purpose: Without persistence, predictions are ephemeral -- no way to assess whether the system is accurate over time. This is the foundation for calibration (Plan 03) and resolution tracking (Plan 02).

Output: PredictionLogger class, prediction_log table, run_id in signal_ledger, brief.py wired to persist predictions.
</objective>
