<objective>
Remove market price anchoring from all prediction model prompts and strip editorial content from crisis context, so models produce independent probability estimates from clean factual inputs.

Purpose: Models currently see market prices before stating probabilities, biasing them toward market consensus. Editorial sections like "What The Market May Be Missing" inject analyst hypotheses that should be the model's own output, not input.

Output: 3 predictor files with market_prices removed from signatures and prompts, crisis_context.py with editorial sections stripped, brief.py and backtest/engine.py with market_context wiring removed, test file verifying crisis context cleanliness.
</objective>
